Frequently Asked Questions

UNI RESIDENTIA welcomes applications from registered students at any accredited South African university. Our current residents attend the University of Pretoria, Wits University, and the University of Johannesburg. You must be enrolled full-time for the academic year you are applying for.

Your monthly fee includes your furnished room, all utilities (water and electricity), high-speed fibre internet, weekly room cleaning, access to all residence amenities (study lounge, gym, recreation zone, laundry), 24/7 security, and community events.

Submit your application through our website. Our team reviews applications within 5 business days. If successful, you'll receive an offer letter with 7 days to accept. Once accepted, your room is secured for the academic year. We recommend applying early as spaces are limited.

Meals are not included in the standard fee. Each residence features a fully equipped communal kitchen where residents can prepare their own meals. The surrounding Hatfield and Braamfontein areas also offer numerous affordable dining options.
