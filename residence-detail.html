About This Residence

The Pretoria Residence is our first and flagship property, setting the benchmark for what premium student accommodation should be. Located in the heart of Hatfield, it places you within a three-minute walk of the University of Pretoria's main campus gates.

Housed in a beautifully restored heritage building with a modern extension, the residence combines timeless architecture with contemporary comfort. Every detail has been considered—from the acoustic insulation in study areas to the ergonomic furniture in every room.

With only 45 rooms across three room types, this is an intimate community of serious students who value their environment as much as their education.

Amenities

  • Rooftop study lounge with city views
  • Ground-floor library and silent zone
  • Collaborative group-work rooms
  • Fully equipped communal kitchen
  • Laundry facilities
  • Secure bicycle storage
  • Courtyard garden
  • Biometric access control
  • 24/7 CCTV monitoring
  • On-site residence manager

Quick Facts

  • Rooms: 45
  • Floors: 4
  • Room Types: Single, Shared, Premium Suite
  • Nearest Campus: UP Main (3 min walk)
  • Transport: Hatfield Gautrain (8 min walk)
  • Security: Biometric + CCTV + Guard
  • Availability: Limited for 2027
